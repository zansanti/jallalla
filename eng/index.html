Jallalla Connections is a social enterprise that, through courses, training in languages, culture, and communication tools, seeks to connect and unite diverse people across Latin America, building bridges of cooperation so different regions can collaborate and contribute to shared projects.

"Jallalla" (pronounced "raiaia" in English) is an Andean word (from Aymara) meaning "breath" (Ja) and "life force" (LLA)—a powerful concept that energizes people in their endeavors. The word "Connections" in portuguese highlights the Brazilian component, creating opportunities to unite those who share this purpose of fostering collaboration and continuing to work across Latin America, providing the tools they need!
