“Jallalla Conexões” is a social enterprise that, through courses and training in languages, culture, and communication tools, seeks to connect and unite diverse people across Latin America, building bridges of cooperation so different regions can collaborate with each other and contribute to projects in common.

"Jallalla" (pronounced "raiaia") is an Andean word (from Aymara) that means "breath" and "life force" (“JA”) and “health and beginnings” (“LLA”) — a powerful concept that encourages people in their projects. The word "Conexões" (“connections” in Portuguese), highlights a Brazilian component, creating opportunities to connect those who share this purpose of bounding collaborations and continuing to work in the Latin America region, and providing the needed tools!
